# FAQ — Weissmann AI Phone Assistant (Switzerland)

## What is Weissmann?
Weissmann is a premium AI phone assistant for restaurants, bars, and cafés in Switzerland. It answers calls, manages reservations, and handles guest inquiries automatically.

## Is Weissmann a SaaS tool?
No. Weissmann is not a self-serve SaaS product. It is a fully managed AI phone service configured by an expert team directly inside each restaurant.

## Why is Weissmann different from other AI assistants?
Unlike typical SaaS tools, Weissmann includes on-site setup, custom call logic, active sales handling on every call, and continuous optimization by a human team.

## What problems does it solve?
- Missed calls during peak hours  
- Phone interruptions for staff  
- Lost reservations  
- Language barriers with international guests  

## Which languages are supported?
German, Swiss German, English, French, and Italian.

## Can it work only during peak hours?
Yes. Weissmann can run 24/7 or only during peak times, evenings, weekends, or as overflow support.

## Is it compliant with Swiss data protection laws?
Yes. Weissmann can be implemented in a DSG- and GDPR-compliant way for the Swiss market.

## Who is it best for?
Restaurants, bars, and cafés in Switzerland that want more reservations without hiring more staff.
